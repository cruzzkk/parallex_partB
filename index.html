Grizzly Bear
  • A grizzly bear is an omnivore. This means it consumes both plants and animals.
  • It eats lots of different things. It will eat salmon and trout. It will also eat nuts, berries, and grasses.
  • It will change its diet throughout the year and eat more before a winter hibernation. This means it goes into a deep sleep in winter.
  • A grizzly bear has no predators. It is an apex predator. This means it is at the top of the food chain.
